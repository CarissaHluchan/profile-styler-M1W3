Outdoor enthusiast taking on the new adventure of becoming a developer.

I wanted a career that would challenge me and continue the learning process while allowing for growth. I learned about coding from my partner and friends. I saw they had continued opportunities for learning in their careers and wanted that for myself so I joined Turing to learn how to code.
